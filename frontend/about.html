About Me

OM

Olamide Miracle

Software Engineering Student

I'm a software engineering student who got tired of forgetting how I solved problems the first time around — so I started writing it all down.

This site is that log, made public. It's mostly notes to my future self: bugs I chased down, things I learned, side projects that went somewhere (or nowhere), and the occasional milestone worth remembering along the way.

I care about writing code that's easy to delete, systems that fail loudly instead of quietly, and tools that get out of the way. Outside of coursework, I'm usually deep in a rabbit hole that started as a five-minute detour.
